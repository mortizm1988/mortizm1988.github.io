👋  I am Marcelo Ortiz, Assistant Professor of Financial Accounting at [Universitat Pompeu Fabra](https://www.upf.edu/en/web/universitat/la-upf-en-els-ranquings). I am also affiliated with the [Barcelona School of Economics](https://bse.eu/) and the [UPF Barcelona School of Management](https://www.bsm.upf.edu/en). I hold a Ph.D. in Finance and an MSc in Financial Engineering from [UAI](https://www.uai.cl/en/); and I visited the [Rotterdam School of Management - Erasmus University](https://www.rsm.nl/) during my doctoral studies. Before my academic career, I worked as a portfolio manager at Itau Bank in Chile for several years.

My [research has been published ](https://www.marceloortizm.com/publications/) in finance (*the Review of Financial Studies*) and management journals  (*Entrepreneurship: Theory and Practice*, and *Global Strategy Journal*). 

Since 2019, I co-organize the [Barcelona Accounting Seminars and Workshops](https://sites.google.com/view/bas-2021/inicio).
