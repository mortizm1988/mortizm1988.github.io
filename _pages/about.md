I am Marcelo Ortiz, Assistant Professor at [Universitat Pompeu Fabra](https://www.upf.edu/en/web/universitat/la-upf-en-els-ranquings). Welcome to my homepage!

I do research in **Financial Accounting** and **Corporate Governance**, with special emphasis on the role of _transparency_ and _family ties_ in the economy and corporate strategies. I am fortunate that my research projects have been published in leading journals, having three publications in the _FT Top 50 journals_. I am also affiliated with the [Barcelona School of Economics](https://bse.eu/) and the [UPF Barcelona School of Management](https://www.bsm.upf.edu/en).

Since 2019, I co-organize the [Barcelona Accounting Seminars and Workshops](https://sites.google.com/view/bas-2021/inicio).

Before my academic career, I was a portfolio manager at Itau Bank, working on interest and liquidity risk management through debt issuance, asset securitization, and derivatives trading.
